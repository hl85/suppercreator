# Writeflow: Stages & Schemas

Detailed documentation on the `outline` and `draft` stages.

## Stage 1: Outline
Distills the central claim and identifies the reader.

### outline.md Schema
- **Angle**: One sentence steer.
- **Reader**: Who, current belief, what they will gain.
- **Core claim**: The single thing the piece argues.
- **Counter-view**: The strongest objection.
